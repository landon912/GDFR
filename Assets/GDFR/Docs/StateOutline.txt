Game States

Setup
	PlayerDraw 1: Star Card Draw to each player, discard leftover
	PlayerDraw 2: Draw 3 goblin cards to each player
	FairyRingDraw: 4 cards fairy side up, discard remaining
	Initiative: Random player starts.
player
	SelectCard: select card to add to fairy ring
	Non-Star play
		flipRhyme: Flip the card in the fairy ring that Rhyme with the players card.
	Star play
		StartPlay:  all cards in fair ring flip
	Collect: collect other cards that match played card's symbol
	VictoryCheck: player has only fairies (any number) face up in hand / At least 6 fairies
